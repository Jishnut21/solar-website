Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our solar and CCTV services

What is the typical payback period for a solar installation?

The payback period typically ranges from 3-5 years depending on your energy consumption, system size, and local electricity rates. Commercial installations often have shorter payback periods due to higher consumption.

Do you provide maintenance services for existing solar systems?

Yes, we offer comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for existing solar systems, including cleaning, performance checks, and component replacements if needed.

Can I monitor my CCTV cameras remotely?

Absolutely! Our modern CCTV systems come with mobile apps that allow you to monitor your property in real-time from anywhere using your smartphone or tablet.

What areas in Tamil Nadu do you serve?

We provide our services across Tamil Nadu, with a strong presence in Villupuram, Ulundurpet, and surrounding districts. Contact us to check availability in your specific location.
